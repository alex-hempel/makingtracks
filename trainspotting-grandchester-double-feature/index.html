Trainspotting: Grandchester Double Feature

Today I caught two trains coming through Grandchester in opposite directions. 2315D and 2309D pulled into Grandchester station waiting for a green signal. The dual track merges into a single track at this point. At the same time, 2308D and 2321D were on their way down from the Little Liverpool Range with a full load of coal.
