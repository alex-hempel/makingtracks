About



This blog is a space for me to post, at random intervals, thoughts and experiences during my foray into model railways. Everything will always be a work in progress.

While I am writing about model railways and my experiences with it, I will not conduct free stealth marketing. So I will only mention the brand of a product I use when it’s appropriate for the context of the article.

My articles, photos and videos are purely for informational purposes and not instructive. Following described processes may not give you the same result.
